Our Goal statement:
→ The CONFAB family believes in connecting people through the simple medium of internet. We believe that people of the same organisation should have no communication gap between them. We respect everyone’s privacy. And that’s why we work to achieve that goal.

→ CONFAB is the website where we connect people of the same organisations. People connect with each other without the risk of their privacy as the user on our website are completely anonymous.
